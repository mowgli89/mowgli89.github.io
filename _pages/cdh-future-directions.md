### Suggested arc
- From one-size-fits-all to **personalized** fetal therapy, matched to a baby's specific biology.
- Refining FETO: better patient selection and understanding the right dose and timing.
- Working *with* the lung's own growth signals rather than only mechanically.
- The longer horizon of molecular and gene-based approaches before birth, and the care and ethics that must come with them.
- The honest framing: these are research directions and hopes, not current offerings.
