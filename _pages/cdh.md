If you have just learned that your baby has **congenital diaphragmatic hernia (CDH)**, you are likely frightened and looking for clear answers. This is a guided series, written in plain language by a fetal surgeon, that walks from the first diagnosis through delivery, surgery, recovery, and long-term life, and ends with the science of why CDH happens and how we are working to do better.

These pages are educational. They are not medical advice and do not replace your own care team. If you have an urgent concern, call your physician or 911.
